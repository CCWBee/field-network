# Field Network Terms of Service (Draft)

This document is a draft. It does not constitute legal advice.

## 1. Overview
Field Network is a marketplace for verifiable real-world observations. Users may post tasks (requesters) and complete tasks (collectors). The platform uses Web3 for authentication and payment rails, but accounts function like a standard marketplace profile.

## 2. Eligibility and Accounts
- You must provide accurate information and maintain the security of your account and wallets.
- You are responsible for all activity associated with your account.

## 3. Marketplace Roles
- Requesters post tasks with a bounty and requirements.
- Collectors submit evidence meeting those requirements.
- Accounts can act as both requester and collector.

## 4. Fees
- Platform fees start at 10% and may decrease to 5% based on account age, reliability, and fulfillment history.
- Fees are deducted from payouts and recorded in the platform ledger.

## 5. Escrow and Payments
- Bounties are held in escrow until acceptance or dispute resolution.
- Partial payouts may apply when a dispute is resolved with a split decision.

## 6. Arbitration and Disputes
- Disputes are resolved via arbitration with evidence review.
- The loser pays the arbitration fee; partial outcomes split the fee.
- Arbitration outcomes can affect reputation and account standing.

## 7. Slashing and Penalties
- The platform may apply penalties (including strikes or stake slashing) for fraud, abuse, or abandonment.
- Excessive penalties may lead to suspension or bans.

## 8. Data Rights and Resale
- The platform obtains a perpetual license to resell task data after a 90-day exclusivity window.
- Collectors receive a royalty on resale as defined by platform policy.
- Requesters receive rights to use task data under the task terms.

## 9. Verification and Quality
- Submissions may be automatically or manually verified.
- Submissions that fail verification may be rejected and subject to dispute review.

## 10. Prohibited Conduct
- No fraud, spoofing, deception, or submission of reused or fabricated data.
- No illegal, unsafe, or harmful tasks or submissions.
- No harassment, abuse, or attempts to bypass platform safeguards.

## 11. Termination
We may suspend or terminate accounts for policy violations or legal risk.

## 12. Changes
We may update these terms with notice in the product or documentation.
