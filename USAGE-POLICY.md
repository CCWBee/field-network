# Field Network Acceptable Use Policy (Draft)

This document is a draft. It does not constitute legal advice.

## 1. Safe and Legal Use
- Tasks and submissions must comply with applicable laws and regulations.
- Do not post tasks that involve trespassing, harassment, or unsafe activity.

## 2. Data Integrity
- Do not submit fabricated, altered, or reused artefacts.
- Do not use GPS spoofing, timestamp spoofing, or data manipulation.

## 3. Privacy and Sensitive Content
- Do not capture personal data without consent.
- Do not submit content involving minors, medical records, or private property access.

## 4. Abuse and Exploits
- Do not attempt to bypass verification, rate limits, or anti-fraud checks.
- Do not abuse disputes or arbitration to delay or avoid payment.

## 5. Enforcement
Violations may result in slashing, strikes, removal of tasks, or account suspension/bans.
