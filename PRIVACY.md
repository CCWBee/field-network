# Field Network Privacy Policy (Draft)

This document is a draft. It does not constitute legal advice.

## 1. Overview
Field Network collects only the data needed to run the marketplace and verify submissions. We do not sell personal data.

## 2. Data We Collect
- Account data: email, username, wallet addresses, and profile details.
- Task data: task requirements, location radius, time windows, and bounty terms.
- Submission data: artefacts, hashes, EXIF metadata, GPS data, and verification outcomes.
- Usage data: logs, device metadata, and security events for abuse prevention.

## 3. How We Use Data
- Deliver and verify tasks.
- Prevent fraud and resolve disputes.
- Improve platform reliability and user experience.

## 4. Data Sharing
- We share data with requesters and collectors as needed to complete tasks.
- We may share data with service providers that enable hosting, storage, or security.

## 5. Data Retention
- Task and submission data may be retained for audit and resale rights.
- Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for platform operations.

## 6. Your Rights
- You may request access to or deletion of your data where applicable.
- You may update profile information at any time.

## 7. Security
We use encryption, access controls, and audit logging to protect data.

## 8. Changes
We may update this policy with notice in the product or documentation.
